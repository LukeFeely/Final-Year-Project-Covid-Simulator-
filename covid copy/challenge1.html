Challenge 1


To understand how viral diseases such as Covid-19 spread it can be useful to first look at things from the perspective of the virus. With this in mind, your first challenge will be to configure the virus in such a way that it kills off over 50% of the population with just 1 initial patient. The Simulator will be at the end of this page and will look like this image:



There will be 150 healthy people moving within the zone on the left. When you click on one, that person will become asymptomatic and turn yellow. The person will remain asymptomatic and moving as normal for a certain period, before developing symptoms and becoming knowingly infected. At this point the person will be removed from the population and move to the quarantine zone in the top right. They will remain there for the duration of their sickness, after which they will either re-enter the population as a healthy person, or they will move to the mortuary on the bottom right.




You will be able to change four aspects of the disease in your attempt to make it as deadly a virus as possible. These are:

  • Probability of Infection - When an infected person comes into contact with a healthy person, how likely are they to transmit the virus. Essentially how contagious the virus is.

  • Fatality Rate - How likely the virus is to kill its host at some point in the duration of the infection.

  • Recovery Period - How long it takes for people to recover from the virus and re-enter the population.

  • Asymptomatic Period - How long after first being infected does it take for symptoms to develop and the person to become aware that they are infected and begin their quarantine.



Your Challenge:


Your challenge is to configure the virus so that it kills over 50% of the population with just one starting patient. Experiment with different values and feel free to try various different things, but try to predict the effects the changes you make might have before you run the simulation again. Set your values using the sliders on the right and click on a person to infect them and create patient zero!! The simulator will run until the disease has died out, at which point the results will be displayed. These simulations are built on probability and the movements of the people is random. This means that the exact same disease could die out instantly the first time, but spread like wild-fire the second time, so don't be afraid to try the same thing more than once.
